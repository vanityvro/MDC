Miso Ramen with Soft-Boiled Eggs

Ramen Noodles

This vibrant noodle stir-fry combines the savory flavors of soy sauce and sesame oil with fresh garlic and ginger, creating a deliciously aromatic base. Tossed with colorful mixed vegetables and cooked noodles, it’s a quick and satisfying dish that’s perfect for any weeknight dinner. Finished with a sprinkle of green onions and sesame seeds, this meal is not only tasty but also visually appealing, making it a delightful choice for both the palate and the eyes. Enjoy your culinary creation!

Ingredients

  • 8 oz noodles (spaghetti, udon, or your favorite type)
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon honey or sugar (optional)
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (like bell peppers, carrots, and broccoli)
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • Sesame seeds for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook the Noodles: Boil a pot of water and cook 8 oz of your favorite noodles (spaghetti or udon) according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Prepare the Sauce: In a small bowl, mix 3 tablespoons of soy sauce, 2 tablespoons of sesame oil, and 1 tablespoon of honey or sugar (if using). Stir until well combined.
  3. Sauté Aromatics:In a large pan or wok, heat a splash of sesame oil over medium heat. Add 3 minced cloves of garlic and 1 tablespoon of minced ginger (if using). Sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Add Vegetables: Toss in 1 cup of mixed vegetables (like bell peppers, carrots, and broccoli) and stir-fry for about 3-5 minutes until they’re tender-crisp.
  5. Combine Noodles and Sauce: Add the cooked noodles to the pan along with the sauce you prepared. Toss everything together, ensuring the noodles and vegetables are evenly coated.
  6. Stir-Fry: Continue to stir-fry for an additional 2-3 minutes, allowing the noodles to heat through and absorb the flavors.
  7. Finish with Green Onions: Add the sliced green onions and stir well to combine, cooking for another minute.
  8. Garnish: If desired, sprinkle sesame seeds on top for added flavor and crunch.
  9. Serve: Plate your delicious noodle stir-fry and enjoy hot!
